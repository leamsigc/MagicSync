# Quick Start

## Creating Your First Post

1. Navigate to the **Scheduler** tab.
2. Click **New Post**.
3. Select the social accounts you want to post to.
4. Write your content and attach media.
5. Click **Schedule** or **Post Now**.

## Connecting Accounts

1. Go to **Settings > Connections**.
2. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want to add.
3. Follow the OAuth flow to authorize MagicSync.
