Find answers to common questions about MagicSync.

## Getting Started

### What is MagicSync?
MagicSync is an all-in-one social media management platform designed specifically for small businesses. It helps you schedule posts, manage multiple accounts, generate content with AI, and track performanceâ€”all from one dashboard.

### Who is MagicSync for?
MagicSync is perfect for:
- **Small Business Owners** who want to save time on social media
- **Restaurants & Cafes** promoting daily specials and events
- **Retail Stores** showcasing products and sales
- **Service Businesses** building trust through testimonials
- **Real Estate Agents** marketing listings
- **Marketing Agencies** managing multiple clients
- **Anyone** who wants to simplify social media management

### How is MagicSync different from Hootsuite or Buffer?
Key differences:
- **Lower Cost**: $29/month vs $60-99/month for competitors
- **Self-Hosted Option**: Own your data completely
- **Unlimited Accounts**: No artificial limits
- **Open Source**: Customize and extend as needed
- **AI Included**: Unlimited AI content generation
- **No Per-User Fees**: Add unlimited team members

### Do I need technical skills to use MagicSync?
No! MagicSync is designed for business owners, not developers. The cloud-hosted version requires zero technical setup. If you choose self-hosting, basic Docker knowledge is helpful but we provide step-by-step guides.

## Technical Questions

### What are the system requirements for self-hosting?
Minimum requirements:
- **CPU**: 2 cores
- **RAM**: 4GB
- **Storage**: 20GB
- **OS**: Linux, macOS, or Windows with Docker

Recommended for production:
- **CPU**: 4+ cores
- **RAM**: 8GB+
- **Storage**: 50GB+ SSD
- **OS**: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or similar

### Can I self-host MagicSync?
Yes! The free version is designed for self-hosting. We provide Docker images and detailed installation guides. See [Installation Guide](/guide/installation).

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We take security seriously:

- **Self-Hosted**: Keep data on your own servers
- **No Third-Party Access**: Your data is never shared
- **Regular Backups**: Automatic backups for cloud-hosted plans
- **SOC 2 Compliance**: Enterprise plans include compliance certifications

### Can I migrate from another platform?

## Troubleshooting

### Why can't I connect my account?
Common issues:
- **Permissions**: Ensure you have admin access to the account
- **API Keys**: Verify your API keys are correct
- **Rate Limits**: Some platforms have connection limits

See [Troubleshooting Guide](/guide/troubleshooting) for detailed solutions.

### Why didn't my post publish?
Check:
- **Scheduled Time**: Must be in the future
- **Account Status**: Account must be connected
- **Content Limits**: Verify character/image limits for the platform
- **Platform Issues**: Check if the platform is experiencing outages

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your email.

### My images aren't uploading. What's wrong?
Verify:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF
- **Size**: Maximum 10MB per image
- **Dimensions**: Recommended 1080x1080 or 1920x1080
- **Connection**: Check your internet connection
