# MagicSync Features

MagicSync is a social media management platform that helps you schedule posts, manage accounts, generate content, and analyze performance from a single dashboard.

---

## Scheduling

![Bulk Import or Create](/img/magicSync-bulkimportorcreate.png)

### Publish to Multiple Platforms

Create a post once and publish it to multiple social networks:
- Facebook
- Instagram
- X (Twitter)
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Google My Business
- Pinterest
- Reddit
- Discord
- Threads
- Bluesky
- WordPress
- Dev.to

**Example:** Write a product announcement once and schedule it to go out on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X at the same time.

### Calendar and Bulk Scheduling

- **Schedule for later** — Plan posts in advance.
- **Bulk upload** — Upload hundreds of posts via CSV.
- **Calendar view** — See all scheduled posts at a glance.
- **Time zone support** — Schedule posts for audiences in different regions.
- **Recurring posts** — Repeat posts automatically on a schedule.

**Example:** Upload a CSV with 30 posts for the month and let MagicSync publish them automatically at the best times.

## Media Library

![Media Library](/img/media-all.png)

The Media Library stores all your images, videos, and other assets in one place.

- Unlimited storage
- Folders and tags
- Built-in image editor
- Bulk uploads
- Support for JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, MP4, MOV, and more

**Example:** Upload all your product photos for the month, organize them by campaign, and attach them to posts directly from the library.

## Growth Strategy and Content Pipeline

The Growth Strategy tool helps you plan, record, and publish content systematically.

### Growth Dashboard

Located at `/app/ai-tools/growth-stratergy`

- **Next Steps** — Checklists for today, this week, and this month
- **Key Principles** — Core guidelines for social media growth
- **Action Plan** — 7, 30, and 90-day plans

### Content Pipeline

Located at `/app/ai-tools/growth-stratergy/create`

1. **Dream and Hook** — Generate ideas and choose a hook style
2. **Record** — Use the built-in teleprompter with speed control and focus mode
3. **Edit Checklist** — Verify hook, value, and call to action
4. **Upload** — Publish or schedule the final video

### Data Analytics

Located at `/app/ai-tools/growth-stratergy/data`

Input metrics such as views, click-through rate, watch time, and engagement rate to receive actionable recommendations.
