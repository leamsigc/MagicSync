### The Cost of Separate Design Tools

Before exploring the editor itself, consider what separate design tools cost your workflow:

- **Subscription Costs:** Canva Pro starts at $13/month. Adobe Creative Cloud starts at $60/month. These costs add up across team members.
- **Export-Import Friction:** Design in one tool, export as PNG, upload to scheduler, realize you need a change, redesign, re-export, re-upload. Each cycle adds minutes that compound across dozens of posts.
- **Version Chaos:** When designs exist as exported files rather than live canvases, tracking which version is "final" becomes guesswork. The wrong version gets published more often than anyone wants to admit.
- **Brand Inconsistency:** Maintaining brand colors, fonts, and templates across separate tools requires discipline that breaks down under time pressure.

MagicSync's integrated editor eliminates all of this. Designs live where they are scheduled. Changes propagate instantly. Brand assets stay consistent.

### Design Tips for Social Media Graphics

- **One Clear Focal Point:** Each graphic should have one dominant element — a headline, a product image, or a face. Do not make the viewer guess where to look.
- **Contrast for Readability:** Text over images requires sufficient contrast. Use semi-transparent overlays behind text or add drop shadows when placing text on busy backgrounds.
- **Consistent Brand Palette:** Stick to 2-3 brand colors plus neutrals. Use MagicSync's color picker to maintain consistency across posts.
- **White Space is Strategic:** Don't fill every pixel. Generous white space around key elements increases perceived quality and improves readability on small screens.
- **Test at Mobile Size:** The majority of social media consumption happens on phones. Zoom out or use the preview to verify readability at small sizes before exporting.
