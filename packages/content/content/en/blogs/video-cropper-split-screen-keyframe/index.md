### Professional Video Editing Without the Desktop Software

Video content dominates social media feeds, but producing polished multi-camera videos usually requires expensive desktop software like Final Cut Pro or Adobe Premiere. MagicSync's **Video Cropper** brings professional-grade cropping, split-screen compositing, and keyframe motion tracking directly to your web browser for free.

---

### Three Core Capabilities

**1. Precision Cropping**
Remove unwanted edges, reframe your subject, or change the aspect ratio of your video to fit platform-specific dimensions. Whether you need a 1:1 square for Instagram, a 9:16 vertical for TikTok, or a 16:9 widescreen for YouTube, our cropper handles it with pixel-perfect accuracy.

**2. Split-Screen Layouts**
Combine multiple video clips into a single frame using customizable split-screen templates. This is ideal for:
- Podcast interviews showing both hosts simultaneously.
- Gaming content with gameplay and facecam side by side.
- Tutorials with screen recording and instructor video.
- Product comparisons showing two products in one frame.

**3. Keyframe Motion Tracking**
Animate your video layers over time using keyframes. Move, scale, or rotate a picture-in-picture overlay across the screen to follow the action. Keyframe controls give you frame-by-frame precision for smooth, cinematic motion.

---

### Step-by-Step: Create a Split-Screen Video

- **Step 1:** Open the **Video Cropper** from the MagicSync Free Tools section.
- **Step 2:** Upload your primary video clip and any secondary clips for the split layout.
- **Step 3:** Choose a split-screen template (side by side, picture in picture, or custom grid).
- **Step 4:** Adjust each clip's crop region, position, and scale within the layout.
- **Step 5:** Add keyframes to animate any layer across the timeline.
- **Step 6:** Preview the result and export as MP4 optimized for your target platform.
